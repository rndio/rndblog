Tutorial
How to get your followers and followings list?
  1. Access to your personal Instagram account
  2. Select the three line icon at the top right corner > Settings and activity
  3. Go to Accounts Center > Your Information and permissions > Download your information > Download or transfer information > Select Instagram Profile > Some of your information > Check on Followers and following > Download to device > Select Date range (All time) > Select Format (JSON) > Create files. See More Information Here!
  4. Wait for the email from Instagram with the link to download your data
  5. Download the file and upload it to this app. Extract the .ZIP in a save location on your computer.
  6. Open the folder and look for the file "followers.json" and "following.json" on the folder "followers_and_following".
  7. Upload the file "following.json" and "follower_1.json" to this app.
